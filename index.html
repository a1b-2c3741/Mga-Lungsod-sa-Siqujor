üõ≥Ô∏è How to get to the Island?

There are different ways in order to get to the Island.

Here are some popular ways:

From Bohol

Ride a Lite Ferry to Larena, Siquijor, departing on Saturday at 7PM. Or, take Ocean Jet from Tagbilaran to Dumaguete (available Monday and Friday only), then book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Cebu

Take a Lite Ferry to Larena, Siquijor, departing Wednesday and Saturday at 2PM. Or take a bus to Liloan, then a Maayo Shipping ferry at 2AM on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday. Daily fast craft to Sibulan is also available, then transfer to Dumaguete and ferry to Siquijor.

From Iloilo

Take a ferry to Bacolod, ride a bus to Dumaguete, then book a ferry to Siquijor.

From Mindanao

From Dapitan or Zamboanga, take a ferry to Dumaguete then to Siquijor. From Plaridel, ride a Lite Ferry to Larena every Sunday at 1PM. You can also reach Siquijor via Cebu City.
